What you're looking at

HashStream shows every transaction on the Hedera mainnet in real time, streamed live from the public mirror node. Each dot is a single transaction flowing across the screen.

The river

  • Every dot is one transaction. It enters on the left and flows right, sorted into a lane by type — crypto transfer, consensus message (HCS), smart contract call, and more.
  • A dot's size reflects the fee paid — larger dots cost more.
  • Tap or hover any dot to inspect its details and open it on HashScan.

The numbers

  • Transactions / sec at the top is live network throughput, averaged over the last 20 blocks.
  • The chips along the bottom track fee revenue, daily pace, the HBAR price, and what's flowed past since you arrived. Hover or tap any chip for a definition.

Controls

  • Ambient switches to a calm, full-screen rain of transactions. Pause freezes the stream, and Fullscreen fills your display.
